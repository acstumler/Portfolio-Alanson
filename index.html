About

I’m an entrepreneur and finance professional based in Louisville, Kentucky, focused on transforming how small businesses understand their financial data. After more than seven years in accounting and advisory — including over 4,000 hours during tax seasons — I saw how much time and clarity business owners lose to manual reporting and disjointed systems. That realization led me to create LumiLedger, a financial software platform that automates data ingestion, classification, and reporting with precision and simplicity.

I grew up in an entrepreneurial family and have always been drawn to building things that make complexity feel simple. My background blends financial expertise with product design thinking — bridging logic, structure, and usability to make powerful tools feel effortless.

When I’m not working on LumiLedger, I enjoy exploring new ideas in software, business architecture, and product storytelling — finding ways to connect strong systems with clean, intuitive experiences.

Now Building: LumiLedger

LumiLedger transforms raw financial data from banks, credit cards, and documents into export-ready statements and KPIs. It replaces manual cleanup with automated ingestion, classification, and journal logic — so owners and advisors can make decisions faster.

What it delivers

  • Transactions, General Ledger, Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Trial Balance, and Cash Flow
  • Two-sided journal entry logic across cash, cards, loans, and equity
  • Exports to Excel, CSV, and PDF

How it works

  • Automated ingestion from statements and feeds
  • Deterministic classification with learned memory
  • Secure, scalable reporting with clean UX
