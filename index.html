About

I’m an entrepreneur and finance professional based in Louisville, Kentucky. I founded LumiLedger to make financial management obvious for small-business owners — turning raw data into clean, actionable insights.

With 7+ years in accounting and advisory, including more than 4,000 peak-season hours, my work spans financial reporting, forecasting, and tax planning. I operate at the intersection of finance and software: clear logic, clean UX, and outputs you can export and use.

In my words

Why entrepreneurship?

I grew up around builders. My grandfather co-founded a company, and my dad and grandfather bought, ran, and sold businesses. That environment made entrepreneurship feel less like a choice and more like something I’m meant to do.

What made you jump?

The LumiLedger idea rolled around for years while I was in financial advising. I reached a crossroads in my career and decided it was time to bring the concept to life.

Early-days advice

Define the problem and remind yourself of it daily. Don’t try to be everything to everyone. Keep moving in a direction. Have conversations, ask questions, build, learn — and ask for help.

Who should start?

Anyone with a real problem to solve and the willingness to surround themselves with people whose skills complement their own. Be sure of yourself. Believe in your ability first.

Now Building: LumiLedger

LumiLedger transforms raw financial data from banks, credit cards, and documents into export-ready statements and KPIs. It replaces manual cleanup with automated ingestion, classification, and journal logic — so owners and advisors can make decisions faster.

What it delivers

  • Transactions, General Ledger, Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Trial Balance, and Cash Flow
  • Two-sided journal entry logic across cash, cards, loans, and equity
  • Exports to Excel, CSV, and PDF

How it works

  • Automated ingestion from statements and feeds
  • Deterministic classification with learned memory
  • Secure, scalable reporting with clean UX
