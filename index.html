About

I'm an entrepreneur and finance professional based in Louisville, Kentucky. I founded LumiLedger to make financial management obvious for business owners by converting raw data into clear statements and insights.

I spent 7+ years in accounting and advisory roles with 4,000+ tax-season hours, working across financial reporting, forecasting, and tax planning. My grandfather co-founded a roofing company in the 1970s, and I grew up around entrepreneurs. Building products that deliver clarity has always felt like the natural path.

  • Clarity over complexity
  • Security and trust
  • Useful over clever

Now Building: LumiLedger

LumiLedger transforms raw financial data from banks, credit cards, and documents into export-ready statements and KPIs. It streamlines data ingestion, automates transaction classification, and generates a Transactions view, General Ledger, Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Trial Balance, and Cash Flow.

  • Automated ingestion and classification
  • Two-sided journal entry logic across accounts
  • Export to Excel, CSV, and PDF
