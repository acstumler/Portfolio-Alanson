Now Building: LumiLedger

LumiLedger transforms raw financial data from banks, credit cards, and documents into export-ready statements and KPIs. It streamlines ingestion, automates classification, and generates the Transactions view, General Ledger, Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Trial Balance, and Cash Flow.

  • Automated ingestion and classification
  • Two-sided journal entry logic across accounts
  • Export to Excel, CSV, and PDF
